## Quick Answer

**MagicSchool AI** is best if you want one platform for everything — lesson plans, rubrics, IEPs, and more. **Diffit** is best if your primary need is differentiating reading materials by level.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | MagicSchool AI | Diffit |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| Tool count | 60+ | 1 (deep) |
| Differentiation | Basic | Best-in-class |
| Lesson planning | ✅ Excellent | ❌ Not available |
| IEP support | ✅ Built-in | ❌ Not available |
| Rubric builder | ✅ Included | ❌ Not available |
| Reading levels | Adjustable | Multi-level output |
| Comprehension Qs | Basic | Auto-generated |
| LMS integration | Google, Canvas | Google Classroom |
| Free tier | Generous | 5/week |

## Pricing Comparison

MagicSchool Pro costs $9.99/month ($119.88/year), while Diffit Pro is $34.99/year — making Diffit significantly cheaper if differentiation is your primary need.

## Privacy

Both vendors publicly present FERPA/COPPA-aligned positioning and school-focused privacy materials. District teams should still review current documentation, contract terms, and implementation details directly with each vendor before approval.

## Who Should Choose What

**Choose MagicSchool AI if:**
- You want one AI login for your entire workflow
- You need IEP drafting, rubric building, or assessment creation
- You're okay with "good enough" differentiation as part of a bigger toolkit

**Choose Diffit if:**
- Differentiation is your biggest daily pain point
- You want best-in-class reading level adaptation
- Budget is tight and you want maximum value for one specific need

## Our Recommendation

For most teachers, **MagicSchool AI** is the better starting point because of its breadth. But if you teach mixed-level reading classes, **Diffit is a must-have supplement** — and at $34.99/year, it's easy to justify both.
