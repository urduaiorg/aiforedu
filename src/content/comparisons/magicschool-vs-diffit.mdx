## Quick answer

**MagicSchool AI** is best if you want one platform for everything — lesson plans, rubrics, IEPs, differentiation, and more. **Diffit** is best if your primary need is differentiating reading materials by level. They solve different problems. Many teachers use both.

## Why this comparison matters

Search results for "MagicSchool vs Diffit" often treat them as direct competitors. They are not. MagicSchool is a broad teacher workflow platform. Diffit is a focused differentiation tool. The right choice depends on the job you need the tool to do.

This page helps educators and school teams decide which one fits their context — or whether both belong on the shortlist.

## Who should choose what

### Choose MagicSchool AI if:

- You want one AI login for your entire workflow
- You need IEP drafting, rubric building, or assessment creation
- You are okay with "good enough" differentiation as part of a bigger toolkit
- Your staff is early in AI adoption and you want one recognizable platform

### Choose Diffit if:

- Differentiation is your biggest daily pain point
- You want best-in-class reading level adaptation
- Budget is tight and you want maximum value for one specific need
- You teach mixed-level reading classes and need multiple Lexile outputs

### Consider both if:

- You want breadth for planning and depth for differentiation
- Your school can support two tools for different use cases
- At $34.99/year, Diffit is easy to justify as a supplement to MagicSchool
