## Quick answer

Choose **Brisk Teaching** when workflow speed inside existing tools matters most. Choose **MagicSchool AI** when your staff wants a broader all-in-one platform that goes beyond feedback into planning, IEP drafting, and more general classroom support.

## The real difference

This comparison is less about which company has more features and more about workflow shape.

- Brisk tends to win on lower-friction feedback and assessment workflows.
- MagicSchool tends to win on breadth and platform coverage.

## Leadership lens

For district leaders, the question is often not "which tool is better?" It is "which tool is easier to govern, explain, train for, and support at scale?"

## When Brisk is the better fit

- You want fast staff adoption
- Feedback and assessment are the main use cases
- You prefer embedded workflow tools over another platform tab

## When MagicSchool is the better fit

- Staff want one platform for many classroom tasks
- You want a broader professional-learning narrative
- The district is willing to support a more expansive tool environment

## Recommendation

If feedback and assessment are the immediate problem, Brisk is often the cleaner starting point. If the district wants a more comprehensive teacher AI platform, MagicSchool AI usually has the stronger case.
