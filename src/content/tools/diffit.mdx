## What Is Diffit?

Diffit is an AI tool that takes any topic, article, or text and instantly adapts it to multiple reading levels. It generates vocabulary lists, comprehension questions, and summaries — all aligned to the adapted reading level.

## Key Features

- **Instant differentiation**: Paste any text or enter a topic to generate versions at multiple Lexile levels
- **Comprehension questions**: Auto-generated multiple choice, short answer, and open-ended questions
- **Vocabulary support**: Key terms highlighted with student-friendly definitions
- **Translation**: Output available in 50+ languages
- **Google Classroom integration**: Push assignments directly to your LMS

## Privacy & Compliance

Diffit publicly presents itself as FERPA-aligned and low-friction for school deployment. AIForEdu reviewed the public documentation, but district teams should still request current privacy terms, implementation details, and approval references before adoption.
