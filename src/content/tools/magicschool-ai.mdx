## What Is MagicSchool AI?

MagicSchool AI is the most popular AI platform built specifically for educators, offering over 60 tools that cover virtually every teacher workflow — from lesson planning and rubric generation to IEP draft writing and parent communication.

## Key Features

**For Teachers:**
- Lesson plan generator (aligned to state standards)
- Differentiation tool that adapts content to reading levels
- Rubric builder with customizable criteria
- Assessment question generator (multiple formats)
- IEP goal writer and accommodation suggestions

**For Students (MagicStudent):**
- Socratic tutor that guides without giving answers
- Essay feedback with specific, constructive suggestions
- Study guide generator from uploaded notes

## Privacy & Compliance

MagicSchool takes privacy seriously. They are FERPA-compliant, COPPA-compliant, and have signed Student Data Privacy Agreements with multiple state-level organizations. Student data is not used for model training.

## Our Verdict

MagicSchool is the best all-in-one AI platform for teachers in 2026. The free tier is genuinely useful, privacy compliance is excellent, and the breadth of tools means you only need one login. The main limitation is that individual tools aren't as deep as specialized alternatives — but for most teachers, the convenience factor wins.
