## What Is MagicSchool AI?

MagicSchool AI is the most popular AI platform built specifically for educators, offering over 60 tools that cover virtually every teacher workflow — from lesson planning and rubric generation to IEP draft writing and parent communication.

## Key Features

**For Teachers:**
- Lesson plan generator (aligned to state standards)
- Differentiation tool that adapts content to reading levels
- Rubric builder with customizable criteria
- Assessment question generator (multiple formats)
- IEP goal writer and accommodation suggestions

**For Students (MagicStudent):**
- Socratic tutor that guides without giving answers
- Essay feedback with specific, constructive suggestions
- Study guide generator from uploaded notes

## Privacy & Compliance

MagicSchool publicly states that it is FERPA-compliant, COPPA-compliant, and offers Student Data Privacy Agreements. The vendor also states that student data is not used for model training. District teams should independently review the current documentation and contract language before rollout.

| Standard | Status |

## Pricing

MagicSchool offers a generous free tier that includes access to most tools with usage limits. The Pro plan at $9.99/month removes limits and adds priority features.

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | 60+ tools, usage limits |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Unlimited use, priority support |
| School | Custom | Admin dashboard, SSO, analytics |

## Our Verdict

MagicSchool is one of the strongest all-in-one AI platforms currently marketed to educators. The free tier is materially useful, the breadth of tools is attractive for school adoption, and the convenience of a single platform is the main advantage. The main limitation is that individual tools are not always as deep as specialized alternatives.

**Rating: 4.7/5** — Best overall AI tool for K-12 educators.
