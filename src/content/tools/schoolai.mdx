## What Is SchoolAI?

SchoolAI provides a managed AI environment where students can interact with AI chatbots while teachers maintain full visibility and control. Teachers create custom "Spaces" with specific AI personas, guardrails, and learning objectives.

## Key Features

- **Custom AI Spaces**: Teachers design AI interactions with specific instructions and boundaries
- **Real-time monitoring**: See every student conversation as it happens
- **Content guardrails**: AI stays on-topic and age-appropriate
- **Assignment integration**: Create AI-powered assignments with rubric alignment
- **Analytics dashboard**: Track engagement, common questions, and learning patterns

## Privacy & Compliance

SchoolAI presents itself as built for the school environment with FERPA and COPPA compliance at its core. Public-facing materials describe teacher and administrator visibility plus student-data protections, but districts should still request current documentation before approval.

## Our Verdict

SchoolAI is the best solution for schools that want to give students access to AI while maintaining appropriate oversight. The teacher monitoring dashboard is excellent, and the ability to create custom AI personas for different assignments is uniquely powerful. The free tier is generous enough for individual teachers to get started.
